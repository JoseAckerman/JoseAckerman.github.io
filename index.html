Covid-19 Blog

Our blog:

This website has been made with the purpose of sharing how it has been for two high school teenagers living in the middle of a huge issue like covid-19, a global pandemic that has changed our way of living. Also was done with the intention of anyone that joins is free to share tips that worked for them to handle these difficult moments, in that way make it like a society looking for help and to help.


We decide to make our website related to this topic because it is what we are living at this moment, it's our new way of living, and we want that if things never came back as it went, be able to remember with details what we loved about the past, or if things came back as it went, we want to remember how things was during this unthinkable situation.


This blog is divided into 4 main pages, Homepage (where you are right now), Portu, José and Our Project, this main page has subpages that will help you to easily find the content you are looking for. The subheadings include personal introduction, experiences, tips and difficulties, online school of each author and information to contact us and give us feedback or any comment you want.
